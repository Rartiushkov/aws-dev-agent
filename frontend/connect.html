sandbox-org / Connect AWS
Connect your AWS organization
Grant read-only access so ShellShift can scan your source environment and plan the migration.
3 steps · ~5 minutes
ShellShift only requires read-only IAM permissions. No write access is needed to scan your source environment.
1
Create an IAM role in your AWS account
Go to IAM → Roles → Create role. Select "Another AWS account" and enter the ShellShift account ID below.
Account ID: 027087672282
2
Attach the read-only policy
Attach the following managed policies to the role. This covers Lambda, SQS, DynamoDB, ECS, and S3 read access.
ReadOnlyAccess
AmazonDynamoDBReadOnlyAccess
AWSLambdaReadOnlyAccess
3
Paste your Role ARN
Copy the Role ARN from the IAM console and paste it here. ShellShift will verify access and begin the inventory scan.

Security
ShellShift never stores your AWS credentials. The IAM role uses cross-account trust — revocable at any time from your AWS console. All scan data is encrypted in transit.
