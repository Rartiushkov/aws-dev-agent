Common questions

What counts as one migration?
One migration is a full source-to-target run: inventory scan, dependency graph, deployment manifest, and validated rebuild on the target account.
Do you need write access to my AWS account?
No. The source account only requires read-only IAM access. Write permissions are only needed on the target account to create resources.
What if the migration fails validation?
You get a full validation report with every issue flagged. We fix issues and re-run at no extra charge until it passes — or we refund.
Can I migrate between regions, not just accounts?
Yes. Pro supports cross-account and cross-region. Enterprise adds multi-account orchestration with a dedicated engineer.
Is there a free trial for Pro?
The Starter plan is free and runs a full migration on sandbox accounts. For production, contact us — we offer a scoping call with a sample report before you pay.
