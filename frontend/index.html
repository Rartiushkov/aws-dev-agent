Three steps. Zero manual work.

Connect your AWS org and let ShellShift handle the rest. From source discovery to validated target deployment.

01
Connect your source account

Create a read-only IAM role and paste the ARN. ShellShift assumes the role and scans your Lambda functions, SQS queues, DynamoDB tables, and ECS clusters. No write permissions needed.

02
We remap every dependency

ShellShift builds a full dependency graph, identifying all resource relationships, environment variables, IAM bindings, and VPC configurations before touching the target.

03
Target rebuilt with evidence

Resources are recreated on the target account in the correct order. Every action generates a deployment manifest and validation report: shareable, auditable, permanent.
